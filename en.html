Economic ideas
Deflation economy
A deflationary economy (an economy with an ever-decreasing number of coins in circulation) can be very profitable to invest.
Super-inflation economy
The economy can be built on super-inflation, when participants receive excess profits from their investments.
Casual games economy
The economy can be built around casual games such as the casino or the lottery. They can be used to control inflation and deflation.
Credits/Mortgages
Loans and mortgages can be the basis of a good market economy and provide an incentive for the growth of the base coin
Limited resources
Limited resources can be the basis of a market economy and provide an incentive for the growth of the base coin
Auctions
Real estate prices and lending rates can be adjusted through user auctions
Price-based feedback
Economic parameters (such as the issuance rate, lending rates, and others) may change depending on the price or its dynamics
Stablecoin-based economy
The economy could be based on an algorithmic stablecoin
No-Coin economy
An economy can be founded without creating additional coins. The value is determined by the value of the assets in the game.
Referral program
You can use the referral program and other techniques to increase the number of users.
DAO-governed economy
Some DAO can control each aspect of your economy.
